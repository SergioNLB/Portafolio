BuildPro

Build Pro is an app designed to connect construction professionals with clients. It features detailed profiles where architects and engineers can showcase their experience and projects. Users can search and explore other profiles, interact on a home wall where services and projects are posted, and add clients to manage contacts and projects. The platform facilitates communication and hiring of construction services, promoting collaboration and project success.
